# Negation-based Economics

## From Hegel to Low-Altitude Air Route Pricing

> The value of an asset does not reside in the asset itself. It resides in its relation of negation to alternatives.

**Negation-based Economics** grounds on Hegel's "determination is negation." Studies how asset value is constituted in relations of negation, applied to low-altitude air route pricing, route insurance, and data assets.
